Greetings from Wrap Magazine
Re-imagining the greeting card was a treat with Wrap. They were so open and wanted playful executions for memorable printed keepsakes that you’d want to stick to your fridge. My favorite part about this project, was walking into stores and seeing them stocked on the shelf.
Nike SB Spring/Summer ‘19
Growing up in Virginia Beach, I always described it to people as a beach city. There was plenty of fake tanning, Valley girl accents and an abundance of skateboarders and surfers in the experience I had. It still holds a special place to me for its uniqueness and post-80's boom architecture.
